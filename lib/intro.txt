Introduction The Dar es Salaam Institute of Technology, DIT, is located in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. It was established to provide higher technical education but could not sustain its original setup and mission. A new institution was necessary to replace the Dar es Salaam Technical College. DIT aims to produce graduates who meet the expectations of Tanzanians through programs in Information and Communication Technology, ICT, and various professional short training programs. The prospectus includes details on academic programs, admission regulations, examination regulations, fees, and academic department profiles. It also covers additional information about the DIT dispensary, accommodation, and catering services. The organizational structure of DIT is outlined to provide a comprehensive overview.
